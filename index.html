Company Overview

Every drop is a promise of performance

Shiv Shakti Oil & Lubricants (SSOL) doesn't just make lubricants — it engineers reliability into every drum. Based in Bhiwadi, Rajasthan, SSOL formulates high-performance engine oils, industrial gear oils, and specialized greases built to take the heat, pressure, and punishment of daily use. No shortcuts, no compromises — every single batch is tested before it ever reaches your machine. From city bikes to industrial giants, SSOL keeps things moving.
